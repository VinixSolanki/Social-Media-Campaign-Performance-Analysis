# Social Media Campaign Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes social media advertising campaign performance across multiple platforms and locations using Microsoft Power BI. The objective is to identify high performing channels, regions, and campaign strategies to support data driven marketing decisions.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Power BI
- DAX
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## Dataset Information
- Time Period: January 2022 to December 2022
- Platforms: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest
- Locations: Miami, Los Angeles, New York, Las Vegas, Austin

### Key Metrics
- ROI
- Engagement Score
- Conversion Rate
- Acquisition Cost
- Impressions
- Clicks

---

## Key Insights
- Instagram and Facebook contribute over 60% of total engagement
- Miami, Los Angeles, and New York generate the highest ROI
- Campaigns running for 45â€“60 days perform better than short duration campaigns
- Pinterest shows relatively lower engagement and needs strategy optimization

---

## Recommendations
- Increase budget allocation for Instagram and Facebook
- Run longer campaign durations for better ROI
- Optimize targeting for low performing locations
- Improve creative strategy for Pinterest
- Refresh creatives during mid year to maintain engagement
